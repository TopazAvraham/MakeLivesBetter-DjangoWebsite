About Us

MLB Community is an enterprise which meant to help those in need, by volunteering and in return getting awesome prizes!

  • Join Our Community by registering our website
  • Search volunteering work you like from the many options on the website
  • Contact the volunteering association and schedule you volunteer meeting
  • Don't forget to take the volunteering approval from the association once your done
  • Upload the volunteering approval to us on the website and get coins
  • Use the coins to get awesome prizes!

We in the MLB Community want to make it simple, fun, attractive and helpful all at a time!

Therefore, the procces for volunteering, helping and getting rewards is very simple!
